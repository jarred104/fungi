Identified Mushrooms Gallery

Check out these fantastic fungi found & identfied (hopefully correctly) in the Pacific Northwest! We are mushroom hunting and photo taking enthusiasts, but not identification experts. Our hope is to build this website into a space where people like us can create their own galleries, maps, and interact with a community of people who share the same passion. Check out our Project Plan page for additional details. In the meantime, enjoy browsing our photos.
