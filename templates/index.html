Interactive Map

Check out our interactive mushroom map! In the future, we hope to build this site so that users can upload information about the mushrooms they've found and track them through their own interactive map. It's a super fun way to remember hikes, road trips, and vacations! Like what you see? We'd love to hear your feedback via email.

Photo Gallery

This page was created as a fun way for us to share photos of the photogenic fungi friends that we've found and (hopefully correctly) identified. Our long-term goal for this page is to create a service where users can enjoy the same functionality and share their photos in a fungi-friendly community.
