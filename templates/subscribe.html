Subscribe

We envision our website as a place where anyone can browse for free, but for a small monthly fee, subscribing members will get the joy of tracking and mapping their finds with "My Mushroom Map," uploading and discussing their photos with the IF community, and sharing their favorite content. Subscriptions will be a modest price (we are thinking just $5). This fee will help keep the trolls away and provides us with some resources to keep this community and website active. We are just two people with an idea and would love to grow this thing into a cool small business. We thought other people who enjoy hunting and photographing mushrooms as much as we do might be willing to pay a small fee for a cool service like this :)

Check out our Project Plan page for a full scope of how we envision the site coming together. In the meantime, enjoy looking over our favorite fungi finds and send us an email if you'd like!
