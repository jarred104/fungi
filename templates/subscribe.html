Subscribe

Anyone can enjoy browsing Ineffable Fungi for free, but subscribing members get the joy of tracking their finds with "My Mushroom Map", uploading and discussing their photos with the IF community, and sharing their favorite content. Subscriptions are just $5 per month. This fee helps keep the trolls away and provides us with some resources to keep this community and website active.

Goals for subscriber functionality:

  • "Create account" and associated login functionality
  • Ability to toggle between community and personal views
